🛡️ Class Specializations & Combat Balance

👑 Paladin

  • Judgement (with Righteous Fury active): Now acts as an active Taunt. Instantly forces the target to attack you, and scales threat generation directly to 110% of the highest threat target (minimum 5,000 threat). Plays a custom Druid Growl/Taunt visual on the target.
  • Holy Shock (Shockadin Spec): Baselines and damage coefficients significantly scaled up, transforming the caster build into a highly competitive Holy DPS spec.
  • Level 18 Starter Pack & Spells: Custom Protection Paladin kit consisting of Soldier's Mail armor set, shield, weapon, 4x 24-slot Portable Holes, and early access to key tanking spells (Righteous Fury, Righteous Defense, and Spiritual Attunement) to enable smooth level-appropriate dungeon runs.
  • Avenger's Shield Recast & Teleport: Re-casting Avenger's Shield while the shield is in-flight teleports the Paladin behind the target and triggers a Shield Burst, dealing high Holy damage to the target and nearby enemies within 8 yards (150 base + 15/level + 200% Int scaling).

🌿 Druid

  • Moonkin Form Mana Recovery: Starfire, Wrath, and Moonfire casts now restore a flat 2% of maximum mana per cast, solving previous caster Druid downtime issues.
  • Revive (Out-of-Combat Res): Automatically learned at level 12 (Spell ID: 50769). Druids now possess a standard non-cooldown resurrection spell.

🔮 Priest

  • Shadowform Mana Recovery: All shadow damage dealt now restores mana equal to 15% of damage dealt. This mana is split and distributed directly to the Priest and all group members within 40 yards.

❄️ Mage

  • Frost Stance (Tank Specialization): Toggling Frost Stance scales Stamina, Intellect, and Spell Power into flat damage reduction and increased threat.
  • Frostbolt Strike: Frostbolt is converted into an instant melee strike. Cast times are automatically canceled during preparation, triggering weapon swings and instant frost damage within 5 yards.

😈 Warlock

  • Metamorphosis (Permanent Toggle): Transforms Metamorphosis into a permanent demon tanking form with boosted armor, threat, and mitigation.
  • Searing Pain: Searing Pain is converted to a fully instant-cast spell. Autocancels spellcast preparation to eliminate spellbar stuttering and double-casts.
  • Nether Swap: Toggling Detect Invisibility swaps positions with your target and triggers a Shadowfury shadow blast/stun at the destination (15-second cooldown).

🏹 Hunter

  • Melee Hunter Spec: Raptor Strike now applies the Agile Hunter buff (+5% melee damage, stacks up to 3 times, lasts 10s). Mongoose Bite's dodge requirement is bypassed; Raptor Strike has a 30% chance to reset the cooldown of Mongoose Bite and cast it instantly.
  • Pet Happiness QoL: Happiness decay is completely disabled. Pets are permanently locked at maximum Happy (+125% damage modifier). Feed Pet is deprecated.

⚡ Shaman

  • Enhancement Haste: Equipping a 2-Handed weapon OR dual-wielding weapons grants a permanent +20% melee attack haste.

🔫 Gunslinger (Subclass)New

  • Three Talent Trees: Opt into custom 31-point trees: Catalyst (elemental procs & static charge), Fortune (crit/luck scaling & roll manipulation), and Munitions (AoE shrapnel & explosive area zones).
  • Ante Mana System: Auto Shot and special ranged shots consume mana to roll for powerful elemental and utility procs.
  • Dynamic Procs: Roll for procs like Lava Shot (Lava Burst), Ice Lance, Lightning Shot (Chain Lightning), Bullet Time (Rapid Fire), or Jackpot (massive Holy blast and self-heal).
  • Database & Addon Sync: Character talents persist in the database and synchronize directly with the custom GunslingerTalents client interface.

⚔️ Spellslinger (Subclass)New

  • Spellsword Specialization: Transforms the Mage into a dynamic melee combatant, combining sword strikes with instantaneous arcane, fire, and frost elemental enhancements.
  • Elemental Enchants: Imbue weapons dynamically to trigger spell effects on hits, generating spell charge resources.
  • Barriers & Wards: Replaces normal cloth shielding with dense physical-warding barriers that absorb damage and convert it into mana or melee haste.
  • Sword Throw: A ranged ability throwing a projected magical sword that pierces enemies in a straight line, dealing spell-scaled damage and pulling the Mage to the final target.
  • Custom Spell IDs: Utilizes a dedicated client/server spell line mapping to custom spell records (IDs `101001` - `101005`).

🏃 ARPG Mobility SystemNew

  • Class Blinks: Using core mobility spells (Sprint, Bloodrage, Fade, Aspect of the Cheetah, Dash, Ghost Wolf, Horn of Winter) instantly blinks the player forward 10-12 yards.
  • Secondary Spell Effects: Blinking triggers class-specific interactions (e.g. Fade triggers a Holy Nova, Bloodrage triggers a Judgment visual).

⚔️ PvP & Dungeon Systems

🗺️ Rogue-lite Dungeon Map SystemNew

  • Consumable Map Items: Roll map items (Item 99205) that designate a target dungeon, keystone level (+1 to +10), and random combat affixes. Use the item in inventory to view a custom details window.
  • Map Chaos Orb: Rerolls the active affixes, quantity, and rarity modifiers of a dungeon map in your bags while preserving its key level and bound dungeon.
  • Loot Quantity & Rarity Scaling: Finishing a mapped dungeon awards upgraded loot. Quantity (+extra items) and Rarity (+quality weights) scale dynamically based on the map's key level and affix count.
  • Map Device: Interact with the Map Device (Creature 99200) next to Chronos in capital cities. It consumes the map, records run parameters on all group members, and warps the party to the instance. (Scales down to 0.35 in-game for a sleeker visual profile).
  • Glimmering Shrines: Spawns an interactable crystalline entity (Creature 99201) at the start of mapped instances or dynamically distributed next to monsters across all open-world zones (over 1,000 active spawns, respawning every 5 min). Select one of four powerful double-edged blessings, which display custom lasting buff icons in your UI:
    • Blessing of the Glass Cannon: +50% Damage Dealt, +50% Damage Taken.
    • Blessing of the Reckless Healer: +50% Healing Done, +25% Damage Taken.
    • Blessing of the Swift Thief: +30% Speed, +15% Damage Dealt, +43% Damage Taken.
    • Blessing of the Fortified Shield: -30% Damage Taken, -30% Movement Speed.
    (Open-world shrine blessings have a strict 2-minute expiration timer).
  • Dungeon Boss Echoes: Transparent, scaled level-appropriate Echo Bosses (Edwin VanCleef, Bloodmage Thalnos, Scarlet Commander Mograine) spawn at leveling hubs. They use a neutral faction, appearing as yellow nameplates so they are ignored by guards/monsters and wait peacefully until players initiate the fight. Defeating them guarantees a dungeon map drop.
  • Boss Combat Affixes: Entering combat with any dungeon boss scales boss health (+8% per key level) and damage, and activates the rolled affix scripts:
    • Volcanic: Eruptions periodically spawn under players.
    • Sanguine: Mobs leave healing pools under themselves upon death.
    • Bolstering: Minions spawn at 75% & 35% health; their deaths buff boss damage by +20% per stack.
    • Explosive: Orbs spawn; must be destroyed within 6s or deal 50% max HP damage.
    • Necrotic: Boss strikes stack a healing reduction (2% per stack, up to 50% max).
    • Quaking: Tremors periodically interrupt and silence spellcast tracks for 5s.
  • Boss Map Drops: Dungeon bosses have a 100% chance (for testing) to drop maps bound to their dungeon. The drop is distributed to all real group members (pets and bots are ignored).

🤖 AI Playerbots & Nemesis System

💀 The Nemesis System (Sworn Rivals)

  • Rival Assignment: Upon reaching level 10, a random level-appropriate playerbot of the opposite faction is designated as your Sworn Rival.
  • Open-World Invasions: Your Rival has a 10% chance every 3 minutes in contested zones to log in, teleport nearby, and attack you. A notification alerts you: "You feel hunted... an invasion is imminent!"
  • Class-Specific Ambush AI: Spawn mechanics adjust based on the bot's class:
    • Rogues & Druids: Teleport in stealth behind the player, follow for 6-10s, and open with Cheap Shot or Pounce.
    • Hunters: Teleport at range (18 yards) and cast Frost/Freezing traps at your feet before engaging.
    • Melee/Casters: Teleport 15 yards away and charge/cast immediately.
  • Rival Progression & Titles: When your rival kills you, they level up, roll dynamic combat traits, and earn custom, rank-appropriate titles (e.g. "The Kingslayer", "The Relentless") announced globally on the server.
  • Dynamic Combat Traits: Higher-level rivals roll up to 3 strengths or weaknesses from a pool of 8 traits:
    • Bloodthirsty: +20% Lifesteal on all damage dealt.
    • Enraged (Low HP): +50% speed and damage below 35% HP.
    • Iron Will: 30% Physical damage reduction.
    • Spell Shield: 30% chance to reflect magic spells.
    • CC Resist: Automatically breaks crowd control every 10 seconds.
    • Arcane Shield (Low HP): Absorbs a shield equal to 25% max health below 20% HP.
    • Vulnerability (Fire/Frost): Takes 50% extra damage from Fire or Frost spell schools.
  • Nemesis Ranks: Defeating or dying to your rival scales their Nemesis Rank from 1 to 5. Higher ranks increase their target stats and enlarge their physical model (+4% size per rank).
  • Exile Orb Loot: Defeating your rival awards active crafting orbs based on their rank: Rank 1-3 awards Orbs of Scouring (at scaling chances up to 100% at Rank 3); Rank 4 awards a 25% chance of an Exalted Orb (otherwise Scouring); and Rank 5 awards a guaranteed Exalted Orb!

⚔️ Dynamic Zone Invasions

  • Zone Assaults: Scheduled or GM-triggered zone-wide PvE invasions in major leveling territories: Westfall (max lvl 20), Redridge (max 25), Duskwood (max 30), Stranglethorn (max 45), Tanaris (max 50), and Silithus (max 60).
  • Themes: Assailing forces roll one of three random themes: Burning Legion Incursion, Scourge Invasion, or Elemental Rift.
  • Phase 1 (Portal Rifts): Spawns 3 high-energy portal rifts across the zone. Each portal is defended by 4 minion mobs and a powerful Elite Portal Guardian. Players must defeat the guardians to close the portals.
  • Phase 2 (Commander Boss): Closing all portals spawns a giant Elite Commander boss (e.g. Doomguard Elite or Abomination Elite) at the zone's center. Defeating the commander successfully repels the invasion and broadcasts a server-wide victory.

💬 AI Personalities & Bounty Hunting

  • Gemini AI Chat: Playerbots whisper you contextually upon defeat or victory, generating customized dialogues based on level, class, and zone.
  • 20 MMO Personalities: Bots chat in General channels and parties under 20 distinct gamer personas (Tryhard, Casual, Sarcastic Troll, Gold Spammer, etc.).
  • Wanted Board visual GUI: Clicking any Wanted Board (GameObject 99100 or original 2713) opens a medieval-themed visual parchment UI rather than a text menu. Track active PvP bot bounties and PvE elite monster hunt contracts, complete with custom gold and Path of Exile crafting orb rewards.
  • Ambush team-ups: Level 30+ players have a 30% chance for a second helper bot to spawn and assist the rival during open-world invasions.
  • Delayed Bot Removal: Non-blocking delay event (2-4s) via Eluna CreateLuaEvent stabilizes server threads during logout transitions, preventing coordinate corruption and crash assertions.

🛠️ Path of Exile Simplified Crafting System

A complete server-side gear enhancement system has been introduced. Right-clicking custom crafting Orbs in your bags opens a dynamic gossip interface showing eligible equippable items (Weapons/Armor) in your inventory. The crafting system has been simplified to only utilize active, high-impact currencies (Exalted, Scouring, Vaal, and Map Chaos Orbs), removing unused legacy orbs.

⚡ Exalted Orb

✨ Double Stat Slamming

Slams a level-scaled random stat enchantment onto any Weapon or Armor (Max 2 slams).

🔗 Fully Linkable Affixes

Slam 1 is placed in Slot 0 and Slam 2 in Slot 5. Both slots are natively serialized, making all slams visible in chat links.

🧹 Orb of Scouring

🧼 Safe Affix Clearing

Wipes all custom crafted slams from Slot 0 and Slot 5, returning the item to its blank state.

💎 Socket & Gem Safety

Scouring is non-destructive and will never clear standard enchants, socketed gems, or native socket colors.

🔮 Vaal Orb

👿 Unpredictable Corruption

Adds a powerful implicit stat to Slot 7 (hidden on chat links). Corrupted items can never be modified or scoured.

🎲 Success vs. Brick

Success (75%): Adds implicit and keeps slams.
Brick (25%): Adds implicit and wipes slams.

⚖️ Tiers & Trade-in

Implicits scale across 4 tiers based on item level. Trade 3x Exalted Orbs for 1x Vaal Orb at any Benches.
